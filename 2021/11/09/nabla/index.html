哈密顿算子运算规则

$\\$

梯度

$$\boldsymbol{T}\nabla=\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{T}}{\partial x^i}\boldsymbol{g}^i\\
\quad\\
\nabla\boldsymbol{T}=\boldsymbol{g}^i\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{T}}{\partial x^i}
$$
散度

$$\boldsymbol{T}\cdot\nabla=\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{T}}{\partial x^i}\cdot\boldsymbol{g}^i\\
\quad\\
\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{T}=\boldsymbol{g}^i\cdot\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{T}}{\partial x^i}
$$
旋度

$$\boldsymbol{T}\times\nabla=\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{T}}{\partial x^i}\times\boldsymbol{g}^i\\
\quad\\
\nabla\times\boldsymbol{T}=\boldsymbol{g}^i\times\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{T}}{\partial x^i}
$$
Laplacian

$$\nabla^2\boldsymbol{T}=\nabla\cdot\nabla\boldsymbol{T}=\boldsymbol{g}^j\cdot
\frac{\partial}{\partial x^j}(\boldsymbol{g}^i\frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}\boldsymbol{T})\\
$$

哈密顿算子的一些等式

$\\$

记$\varphi$为标量场函数，$\boldsymbol{u},\boldsymbol{v}$为矢量场函数，$\boldsymbol{T}$为张量场函数，下文相同

$$\nabla(\varphi\boldsymbol{T})=(\nabla \varphi)\boldsymbol{T}+\varphi(\nabla\boldsymbol{T})$$

$$\nabla\cdot(\varphi\boldsymbol{T})=(\nabla \varphi)\cdot\boldsymbol{T}+\varphi(\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{T})$$

$$\nabla\times(\varphi\boldsymbol{T})=(\nabla \varphi)\times\boldsymbol{T}+\varphi(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{T})$$

$$\nabla(\boldsymbol{u}\cdot\boldsymbol{v})=(\nabla\boldsymbol{u})\cdot\boldsymbol{v}+(\nabla\boldsymbol{v})\cdot\boldsymbol{u}$$

$$\nabla(\boldsymbol{u}\cdot\boldsymbol{v})=\boldsymbol{u}\times(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{v})+\boldsymbol{v}\times(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u})+\boldsymbol{u}\cdot(\nabla\boldsymbol{v})+\boldsymbol{v}\cdot(\nabla\boldsymbol{u})$$

$$(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u})\times\boldsymbol{v}=(\boldsymbol{u}\nabla-\nabla\boldsymbol{u})\cdot\boldsymbol{v}$$

$$\nabla\times(\boldsymbol{u}\times\boldsymbol{v})=\boldsymbol{u}(\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{v})-\boldsymbol{v}(\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{u})-\boldsymbol{u}\cdot(\nabla\boldsymbol{v})+\boldsymbol{v}\cdot(\nabla\boldsymbol{u})$$

$$\nabla\times\nabla\varphi=0$$

$\nabla$证
$$\begin{aligned}\nabla\times\nabla\varphi&=\boldsymbol{g}^i \times \frac{\partial(\boldsymbol{g}^j \frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial x^j})}{\partial x^i}\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i \times(\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i}\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \frac{\partial ^2 \varphi}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i \times(-\boldsymbol{g}^k \Gamma_{ik}^j \frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \frac{\partial ^2 \varphi}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &=0 \end{aligned}$$

$$\nabla\cdot(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u})=0$$

$\nabla$证
$$\begin{aligned}\nabla\cdot(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u})&=\boldsymbol{g}^i \cdot \frac{\partial(\boldsymbol{g}^j \times \frac{\partial\boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j})}{\partial x^i}\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i \cdot(\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i}\times\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \times\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i \cdot(-\boldsymbol{g}^k \Gamma_{ik}^j \times\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \times\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &=(\boldsymbol{g}^i \times-\boldsymbol{g}^k \Gamma_{ik}^j) \cdot\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+(\boldsymbol{g}^i \times\boldsymbol{g}^j)\cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}\\\\ &=0 \end{aligned}$$

$$欧氏空间中有\\
\quad\\
\nabla\times(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u})=\nabla(\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{u})-\nabla\cdot(\nabla\boldsymbol{u})$$

$\nabla$证
$$\begin{aligned}&\quad\nabla\times(\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u})-\nabla(\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{u})+\nabla\cdot(\nabla\boldsymbol{u})\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i \times \frac{\partial(\boldsymbol{g}^j \times \frac{\partial\boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j})}{\partial x^i}-\boldsymbol{g}^i \frac{\partial(\boldsymbol{g}^j \cdot \frac{\partial\boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j})}{\partial x^i}+ \boldsymbol{g}^i \cdot \frac{\partial(\boldsymbol{g}^j \frac{\partial\boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j})}{\partial x^i}\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i \times(\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i}\times\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \times\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}) -\boldsymbol{g}^i (\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i}\cdot\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &\quad+\boldsymbol{g}^i \cdot(\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i} \frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+\boldsymbol{g}^j \frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &=\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i}\boldsymbol{g}^i\cdot\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+ \boldsymbol{g}^j\boldsymbol{g}^i\cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}-\boldsymbol{g}^i\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{g}^j}{\partial x^i}\cdot\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}-\boldsymbol{g}^i\boldsymbol{g}^j \cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}\\\\ &=-\Gamma_{ik}^{j}\boldsymbol{g}^k\boldsymbol{g}^i\cdot\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}+ \boldsymbol{g}^j\boldsymbol{g}^i\cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}+\Gamma_{ik}^{j}\boldsymbol{g}^i\boldsymbol{g}^k\cdot\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j}-\boldsymbol{g}^i\boldsymbol{g}^j \cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^j\boldsymbol{g}^i\cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}-\boldsymbol{g}^i\boldsymbol{g}^j \cdot\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j}\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i\boldsymbol{g}^j\cdot(\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^j \partial x^i}-\frac{\partial ^2 \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial x^i \partial x^j})\\\\ &=\boldsymbol{g}^i\boldsymbol{g}^j\cdot\boldsymbol{u}\cdot\boldsymbol{R}:\boldsymbol{g}_i\boldsymbol{g}_j \end{aligned}$$

正交曲线坐标系哈密顿算子的基矢量表示

$\\$

记$\boldsymbol{r}=\boldsymbol{r}(x^1,x^2,x^3)$表示三维空间一点的矢径，$x^i$称为该点的曲线坐标，$\boldsymbol{g}_i=\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{r}}{\partial x^i}$为该点的协变基矢量，$\boldsymbol{e}_i$为单位协变基矢量，$|\boldsymbol{g}_i|=A_i$为$\textbf{Lam}\acute{\textbf{e}}$常数，$u\langle i\rangle$为矢量$\boldsymbol{u}$在单位协变基矢量下的坐标分量，$\partial_i$表示对坐标$x^i$的普通偏导数$\frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}$

$$\nabla \varphi=\sum\limits_{i=1}^3 \frac{\boldsymbol{e}_i}{A_i}\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial x^i}$$

$$\nabla\cdot \boldsymbol{u}=\frac{1}{A_1 A_2 A_3}\sum\limits_{i=1}^3 \frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}(\frac{A_1 A_2 A_3}{A_i}u\langle i\rangle)$$

$$\nabla\times\boldsymbol{u}=\frac{1}{A_1A_2A_3}{\left|\begin{array}{cc}
A_1\boldsymbol{e}_1 & A_2\boldsymbol{e}_2 & A_2\boldsymbol{e}_2 \\
\partial_1 & \partial_2 & \partial_3 \\
A_1u\langle 1\rangle & A_2u\langle 2\rangle & A_3u\langle 3\rangle\\
\end{array}\right|}$$

$$\nabla^2 \varphi=\frac{1}{A_1 A_2 A_3}\sum\limits_{i=1}^3 \frac{\partial}{\partial x^i}(\frac{A_1 A_2 A_3}{A_i^2}\frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial x^i})$$
